devMeet

Services

devMeet is a platform designed to help indie game developers meet and

work with each other. Creating a video game requires many skills, such as

programming, art, modeling, animation, music, and more. With devMeet,

people with different talents can form teams and achieve bigger goals.

devMeet offers social networking between developers by having users

create accounts. Users can then search for teammates with specific

criteria. They can then invite said user to a team. devMeet also offers a

chat feature between teammates.
